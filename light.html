Seeing into the Light

Morristown NJ
July 19-20, 2019

Come Join me for a mini-retreat this July 19 and 20th we will be exploring the ways that leaning into our experiences of pain or suffering bring us through to the other side of an experience - into the light.

We will be using the challenging and painful, uplifting and life affirming stories of Viktor Frankl, Sir Lawrence Van Der Post, and Jacques Lusseryan among others to inspire us and help us to reinterpret our struggles and pain in transformational ways.

We will be meeting for the evening on Friday and all day Saturday to explore these stories and apply the collected wisdom to our own.
